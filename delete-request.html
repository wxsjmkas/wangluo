Data Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your data or account associated with the Selamat application, please follow the steps below.

How to request deletion:

Please send an email to: wangluo678@proton.me

Subject: Data Deletion Request - Selamat

Please include: Your Device ID or any relevant identifiers used within the app.

What data will be deleted?

  • Personal support history and emails.
  • Feedback logs and technical identifiers associated with your device.
  • Any cached configuration data (if applicable).

Retention & Timeline

Upon receiving your request, we will process the deletion within 7 business days. Please note that some anonymous technical logs or billing records may be retained for the minimum period required by local laws for tax and audit purposes.
